Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and billing.

Are the cleaning agents you use safe for children and pets? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, absolutely. We prioritize the health of your family. Our cleaning solutions are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and specifically selected to be safe for both children and pets while still delivering a powerful, deep clean.

How long will it take my carpet to dry? keyboard_arrow_down

Typical dry time is between 4 to 6 hours. This can vary depending on the airflow, temperature, and humidity in the home. We use powerful extraction equipment to remove as much moisture as possible to speed up this process.

Why is professional cleaning important? keyboard_arrow_down

Professional cleaning not only revitalizes the appearance of your carpets and upholstery but also extends their lifespan. It removes deep-seated allergens, dust mites, and bacteria that regular vacuuming cannot reach, contributing to a healthier home environment.

How often should I have my carpets cleaned? keyboard_arrow_down

Most manufacturers recommend professional cleaning every 12 to 18 months to maintain warranty coverage. However, if you have pets, children, or high foot traffic, we recommend scheduling a cleaning every 6 to 12 months.

Can you remove all stains? keyboard_arrow_down

We treat all stains with advanced, specialized solutions and have a very high success rate. However, some stains may be permanent depending on the substance, the fabric type, and how long the stain has been set.

Do you handle pet odors? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we specialize in neutralizing pet odors. We use enzymatic cleaners that break down the odor-causing bacteria at the source, rather than just masking the smell, to leave your home smelling truly fresh.
